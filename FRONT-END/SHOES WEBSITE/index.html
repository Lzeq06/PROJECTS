WebAbout

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports and officially becoming Nike, Inc. in 1971, the company is headquartered near Beaverton, Oregon. Its mission is simple yet profound: "To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world" — adding the caveat that "if you have a body, you are an athlete." Through its iconic "Just Do It" slogan and partnerships with legends like Michael Jordan, Serena Williams, and Cristiano Ronaldo, Nike has transcended sport to become a dominant force in global fashion and street culture.
